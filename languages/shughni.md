# Shughni — NLP Resources

[← Back to main list](../README.md)

Shughni is an **endangered** Iranian language of the Pamir Mountains, spoken by fewer than 100,000 people primarily in the Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Region of Tajikistan and the Badakhshan Province of Afghanistan. It belongs to the Southeastern Iranian branch and has extremely limited digital resources.
