# Dari — NLP Resources

[← Back to main list](../README.md)

Dari is a variety of Persian spoken in Afghanistan, serving as one of the country's two official languages. While closely related to Iranian Persian (Farsi), Dari has distinct vocabulary, pronunciation, and some grammatical features. Together with Persian and Tajik, these three languages are spoken by over 110 million people worldwide. Computational linguistics work for Dari remains extremely limited, with most NLP research focusing on the Iranian standard.
