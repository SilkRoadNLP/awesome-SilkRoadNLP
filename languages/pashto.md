# Pashto — NLP Resources

[← Back to main list](../README.md)

Pashto is the co-official language of Afghanistan, spoken by approximately 40-60 million people across Afghanistan and Pakistan. Despite its significance, Pashto has received limited research attention in computational linguistics compared to Persian, and remains substantially under-resourced.
