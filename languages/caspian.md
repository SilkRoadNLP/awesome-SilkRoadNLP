# Caspian Languages: Gilaki & Mazandarani — NLP Resources

[← Back to main list](../README.md)

**Gilaki** and **Mazandarani** (also known as Tabari) are Indo-Aryan languages spoken in regions adjoining the Caspian Sea in northern Iran. Mazandarani is spoken by 2.5–3 million people in Mazandaran Province, while Gilaki is spoken by over 2 million people in Gilan Province. Despite their substantial speaker populations, computational resources for these languages remain extremely limited.

## Script Normalization

| Paper | Authors | Year | Links |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| [Script Normalization for Unconventional Writing of Under-Resourced Languages in Bilingual Communities](https://aclanthology.org/) | Ahmadi et al. | 2023 | [![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Code-black?logo=github)](https://github.com/sinaahmadi/ScriptNormalization) [![HuggingFace](https://img.shields.io/badge/🤗-Demo-yellow)](https://huggingface.co/spaces/SinaAhmadi/ScriptNormalization) |

> Addresses script normalization for the Perso-Arabic scripts of Mazandarani and Gilaki alongside other languages. Uses transformer-based encoder-decoder models (JoeyNMT) at the character level. The script normalization work provides fundamental preprocessing for any future NLP development in these languages.
